# SMS AI Chatbot Service

This is an SMS-based AI chatbot service that allows users to interact with an AI assistant by sending text messages to a designated phone number.

## What is this service?

When you text our number, your message is received and processed by an AI assistant, which then replies directly to your phone via SMS. No app download or account creation is required.

## Consent and Opt-In

By texting our SMS number, you agree to the following:

- You are voluntarily initiating contact with this service
- You consent to receive AI-generated SMS responses to your messages
- This is a proof-of-concept project for development and testing purposes only
- Your messages may be used to improve the service
- You can opt out at any time by replying **STOP**

## How to Use

1. Text any message to our SMS number
2. The AI assistant will respond to your message within seconds
3. Continue the conversation naturally as you would with any messaging service
4. Reply **STOP** at any time to opt out

## Tech Stack

- Python
- Twilio (SMS gateway)
- Claude AI (Anthropic)
- Flask / FastAPI
- Ngrok (local development tunneling)
